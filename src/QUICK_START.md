## Common Questions

### Q: How often should I use this tool?

**A:** Daily for news (15-20 min), Weekly for deep dives (2-3 hours)

### Q: Which analysis depth should I use?

**A:**

- **Prelims preparation**: Basic is sufficient
- **Mains preparation**: Use Advanced
- **Interview prep**: Definitely Advanced

### Q: How many articles should I bookmark daily?

**A:** Quality over quantity! 3-5 well-analyzed articles > 20+ skimmed articles

### Q: Can I use this offline?

**A:** Not currently. This is a web-based tool requiring internet.

### Q: Are the analyses accurate for UPSC?

**A:** The demo uses simulated analysis. With real AI integration (see INTEGRATION_GUIDE.md), accuracy depends on the AI model used (GPT-4 recommended for best results).

### Q: Can I share articles with study partners?

**A:** Click "Share" on any analysis page (feature shows UI, needs backend integration for full functionality)

### Q: What if I find an error in the analysis?

**A:** Always verify information from official sources. This tool is meant to aid your preparation, not replace primary sources.
